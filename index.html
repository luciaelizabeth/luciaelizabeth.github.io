--- layout: page title: "About" --- I am Assistant Professor in the Computational Linguistics and Text Mining Lab (CLTL) at Vrije Universiteit (VU) Amsterdam. My research focuses on natural language understanding with the broad goal to develop computational models capable of understanding language in human-like ways. I'm currently focusing on projects related to situated language understanding in human-robot dialogue, developing an Interactive Cookbook dialogue system, and generally asking how symbolic representations can facilitate these tasks.
